Ayurveda believes health is based on balance of Vata, Pitta, and Kapha.

Dry skin and anxiety are symptoms of Vata imbalance.

Excess heat, acidity, and anger indicate Pitta imbalance.

Heaviness, laziness, and congestion are signs of Kapha imbalance.

Vata can be balanced with warm foods, oil massage, and regular sleep.

Pitta can be reduced with cooling foods like cucumber and coconut.

Kapha can be reduced with exercise and light, spicy foods.
